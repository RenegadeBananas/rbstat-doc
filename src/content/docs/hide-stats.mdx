If you use a [FStatModifier](/structs/fstatmodifier) in the editor, you will see in StatName your stats, but also example ones from the plugin.

<img src="../../assets/stat-name-all.png" alt=""/>

### How to hide other stats

<Steps>
	<ol>
		<li>
			<b>Go in Project Settings > Plugins > RBStat</b>
		</li>
		<li>
			<b>Select your stat component parent class</b>
			
			<img src="../../assets/stat-name-setting.png" alt=""/>
		</li>
		<li>
			<b>Now only the stats of your parent class and derived classes are shown</b>
			
			<img src="../../assets/stat-name-hidden.png" alt=""/>
		</li>
	</ol>
</Steps>
