To change the formula that determines the level according to experience in a [UExperienceStat](../classes/uexperiencestat), you will need to override its [GetExperienceRequired](../classes/uexperiencestat#GetExperienceRequired)() function.

## Blueprint

<Steps>
	<ol>
		<li>
			<b>Right click in the Content Browser</b>
			
			Select <b>Blueprint Class.</b>
			<img src="../../assets/bp_create_class.png" alt=""/>
		</li>
		<li>
			<b>Pick parent class</b>
			
			In <b>All classes</b>, pick <b>ExperienceStat</b>.
			<img src="../../assets/bp_create_experience.png" alt=""/>
		</li>
		<li>
			<b>Open your experience stat blueprint</b>
		</li>
		<li>
			<b>Mouseover the right side of the function panel to see the hidden menu "Override"</b>
			
			Override GetExperienceRequired().
			<img src="../../assets/bp_override_experience.png" alt=""/>
		</li>
		<li>
			<b>Change the formula by what you desire</b>
			
			<img src="../../assets/bp_formula_experience.png" alt=""/>
			<Aside type="caution">Be sure that your formula is valid for InLevel == 0, even if unused.</Aside>
		</li>
	</ol>
</Steps>
